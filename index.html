Hello reader! I'm Lex- I'm 26 years old and from Seattle. I live in the Capitol Hill neighborhood with my wife and our three dogs.
I was born and raised here in Washington, but just moved to the big city two and a half years ago. I can't imagine ever going back to the suburbs!

Why programming?

As I mentioned- I love the city. Unfortunately, city living isn't cheap. I wanted a chance to break into an industry that would really increase my earning potential and allow me to flex my creative muscle.
Traditional college has never been for me, but I thrive in a hands on learning environment. I'm really looking forward to learning such a valuble new skill.
I also hope to show other people socialized as girls and women that access to the tech world is possible for us!
